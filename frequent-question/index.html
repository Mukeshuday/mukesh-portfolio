Frequent Asked Questions?
What is this project about?
This project is designed to help users manage their tasks efficiently by providing a simple and intuitive interface. It ensures productivity and smooth collaboration for individuals and teams.
How do I sign up or register?
Click on the “Sign Up” button on the homepage, fill in your details, and submit the form. A verification email will be sent to activate your account.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click “Forgot Password?”, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email we send to reset your password.
Is this project free to use?
Yes, the basic features are completely free. We also offer premium plans with additional tools and storage options.
What platforms are supported?
You can access this project on the Web, and we also offer apps for both Android and iOS platforms.
